Introducing the new TRANSLATE tag that makes your email communication more dynamic and versatile. You can now easily translate text within your emails to different languages on the fly.

Syntax:

{TRANSLATE('text', 'source_lang_code', 'target_lang_code')}

Example:

{TRANSLATE('Hello, world', 'en', 'fr')} = Bonjour le monde

Language Codes:

  • Japanese: ja
  • English: en
  • Spanish: es
  • French: fr
  • German: de
  • Chinese: zh
